E-Ration Shop

The main purpose of this site is to help users get details related to their ration shop. It can avoid dealer communication, manual calculation, and reduce time spent in the shops. The main reason for using this website is to make this process computerized and to remove the drawbacks of the present way of issuing products based on ration cards. The main drawback of the current system is that the PDS has been criticized for its urban bias and its failure to serve the poorer sections of the population effectively. Also, many retail shopkeepers have a large number of bogus cards to sell food grains in the open market. Many FPS dealers resort to malpractice since they acquire less salary. By this project, we are solving this problem too.

Features:

  • Add Multiple Customers
  • Admin Manage Shop Keeper And Customers
  • Shop Keepers Can Add Items
  • Add Complaints
  • Reply Complaints
  • Payments
