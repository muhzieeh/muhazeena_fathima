Career Guidance and College Admission Prediction System

This system provides career guidance and predicts admission chances for colleges based on various factors. It assists students in making informed decisions about their future education and career path.

Features:

  • Admin Manage College, Student, Agency, Expert
  • College and Student Can Register
  • Predict the College for Student
  • Can Choose the Better Career
  • Add Complaints
  • Reply to Complaints
  • See Result, Trends, Request Status, etc.
