Text-to-Speech

Text-to-speech (TTS) technology stands at the forefront of a transformative era in humancomputer interaction, revolutionizing the way we engage with written content. This innovative application harnesses the power of sophisticated algorithms to seamlessly convert written text into natural-sounding spoken words, breaking down barriers for accessibility and communication.

Features:

  • user enter the Text
  • Speak The Audio
  • User Choose The Accent
  • User Choose The Speed
  • User Can Save The Audio
